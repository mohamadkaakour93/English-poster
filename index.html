Some tips to have a better English level:

  1. Always force yourself to speak in English while you are on campus.

  2. Try to learn new vocabulary on memrise (10 words per day).

  3. Listen to the song of the day while reading the lyrics.

  4. Watch some movies with English subtitles.

  5. Practice practice practice! And don't forget to have fun while you learn!
